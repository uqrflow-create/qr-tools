Why QR Code Contrast Matters

Low contrast colors can make QR codes difficult or impossible to scan. Most QR scanners perform best when there is a strong contrast between the QR pattern and the background.

Black on white provides the highest reliability, while similar colors such as gray on white or blue on black may reduce scan performance.
